Who are we?

We are a team of Undergraduate students from the University of York working on a Software Engineering Project.

What is our project?

Our project is to deliver a 2D game. The game would be based off of the York Dragon Boat Race Challenge. A player would play through different legs of a boat race, playing against bots and avoiding obstacles in the river.

Here is a link to our GitHub repository: https://github.com/Frinksy/dragon-boat-race-17/
